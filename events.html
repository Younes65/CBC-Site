FAQs

What is Czech Business Council?
The Czech Business Council is an association of Czech businessmen active in the United Arab Emirates. Its goal is to create a platform and background for a meeting of Czech companies active in the UAE with official bodies of the Czech Republic in the UAE, to create favorable conditions for sharing information and experiences that are vital for doing business in the UAE, to support Czech companies that are to enter the UAE market, to help develop bilateral business and cultural relations, economic activities, investments and other initiatives between the Czech Republic and the UAE, to organize events to promote an image of the Czech Republic in the UAE.
How does the Czech Business Council Perform its role?
The Czech Business Council in Dubai was officially founded on May 29th in 2014 with support of the Embassy of the Czech Republic to the UAE and Czech Trade, the national export promotion organization of the Czech Republic Czech Business Council has been also granted support by the Ministry of Industry and Trade of the Czech Republic and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Czech Republic. Czech Business Council has recently finalized all processes that are needed for the registration with the Dubai Chamber.
What Credibility does the Czech Business Council have?

The CBC operates under the umbrella of the Dubai Chamber, and is fully supported by the Czech Embassy located in Abu Dhabi in the UAE and the Czech Trade the official channel of the business between Czech Republic and UAE.
What makes the Czech Business Council unique at what they do?
CBC is a amazing source of experience and information in doing business in the MENA region, it has wide network of contacts among businessmen and government official. Members of the Czech business Council benefit from association’s strong reputation, hard work environment and members special offers.
How can one become a CBC Member?
The CBC offers many categories of membership – individual, corporate, and honorary. An application form for membership may be found at the top slider of the home page.
